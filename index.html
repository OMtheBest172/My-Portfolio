Research Papers

Here are some research papers I wrote

1.Interaction of Laser Diffraction Intensity Through High Voltage Plasma Interaction: An Experimental Study

This experiment investigates the effect of plasma generated at a single slit on the diffraction pattern of a laser beam. The results indicated a very slight deflection in the intensity of fringes when plasma was introduced. This tells us that the presence of ionized gas alters the refractive index in the region of the slit. This may be due to the fact that plasma might be inducing oscillations or disturbances within the laser‚Äôs interaction zone.

This paper is also published in Plasma Physics eJournal (SSRN), Vol. 2, Issue 45

2.A Framework for Creative Problem-Solving in AI Inspired by Neural Fatigue Mechanisms

This paper introduces a fresh theoretical framework for creating AI systems based on Spiking Neural Network (SSN) Architecture, designed to match human-like creative problem-solving. This framework utilizes the principle of Neural Fatigue caused by saturation of commonly used neural pathways which forces it to use the underutilized neural pathways that facilitate creative solutions.

This paper is also published in Computing Methodology eJournal Vol 8, Issue 86
